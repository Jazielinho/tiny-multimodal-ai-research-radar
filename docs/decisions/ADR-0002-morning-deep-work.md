# ADR-0002: Morning Deep Work

## Status

Accepted.

## Context

The schedule includes work from 8:00 to 15:00, CrossFit from 15:00 to 18:00, family time, meetings and summer heat.

## Decision

Deep AI work should happen mainly in the morning.

Default deep-work window:

```text
06:00 - 07:15
```

## Consequences

- Nights are protected for light work, English, reading and recovery.
- The project becomes more realistic and sustainable.
- If the morning fails, the fallback is the minimum mode: 25 minutes.
